What is it?

deTpression is a device that monitors the state of the patient and estimates the depression severity as well as the risk of having a depressive episode.

It moonitors the patient’s voice and face and identifies his state using our machine learning algorithm. The estimation is reported as a PHQ-9 score, which is a standardised measurement for evaluating depression severity.

What We Do

  • We allow the overbooked phychiatrists to take more patients by offering them the possibility to remotely monitor patients with little to no overhead.
  • Provide a low-cost preventive treatment for the patient.
  • Solve the stigma associated with depression, by reducing the visits to the specialist cabinet.
  • Provide a user friendly interface, that offers useful information about the patient's state and evolution.

How it works?

The patient is recorded, the information is sent to the cloud for being processed and the result of the analysis is sent to the server and displayed in the interface where the doctor can access it.
